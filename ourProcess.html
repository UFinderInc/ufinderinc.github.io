THE PROCESS

Data Collection

UFinder collects data from universities and instisutions from all over the world, ranging from requirements to rankings. Our partnerships with top universites around the world allow us to gather useful data from around the world.

Student Usage

UFinder takes the data gathered from universites and aggregates it in an easy to understand, and useful format to help university students decide on the university of their choice.

Commissions

UFinder receives a commission from universites for students who register for their university using UFinder.
