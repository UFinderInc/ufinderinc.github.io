Features

The World’s Largest Post-Secondary Admissions Database.

UFinder has the world’s largest database of post-secondary admission requirements. It has everything a prospective student needs to get into the post-secondary program of their dreams, featuring:

  • minimum and average admission averages
  • required prerequisites
  • standardized test requirements (SAT, ACT)
  • Advanced Placement opportunities

Personalized University Recommendations.

UFinder uses a smart algorithm that incorporates various factors from the student’s UFinder profile, such as:

  • school system
  • program(s) of choice
  • location preference
  • grades and other qualifications
  • student budget
  • standardized tests

Complied Ranking System.

UFinder collects university rankings from across the web, from reputable sites such as Maclean’s and The Guardian. These are then weighted into our algorithm to recommend student the best schools for them.
