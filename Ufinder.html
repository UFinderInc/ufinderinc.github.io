Our Product

UFinder is a searching tool aimed for high school students, who want to maximize their chances to access the best Universities, based on their situation. Instead of wasting time seeking for help on forums, searching for the tuition fees on each University website while keeping an eye on rankings and the applications deadline, UFinder does all this workfor them. Users(students) can input their own information and our matching algorithm will advise them with the Universities that correspond the best to their ability and academic/career goals.

Features

The World’s Largest Post-Secondary Admissions Database.

UFinder has the world’s largest database of post-secondary admission requirements. It has everything a prospective student needs to get into the post-secondary program of their dreams,featuring:

  • minimum and average admission averages
  • required prerequisites
  • standardized test requirements (SAT, ACT)
  • Advanced Placement opportunities

Personalized University Recommendations.

UFinder uses a smart algorithm that incorporates various factors from the student’s UFinder profile, such as:

  • school system
  • program(s) of choice
  • location preference
  • grades and other qualifications
  • student budget
  • standardized tests

Complied Ranking System.

UFinder collects university rankings from across the web, from reputable sites such as Maclean’s and The Guardian. These are then weighted into our algorithm to recommend students the best schools for them.

The Process

Data Collection

UFinder collects data from universities and instisutions from all over the world, ranging from requirements to rankings. Our partnerships with top universites around the world allow us to gather useful data from around the world.

Student Usage

UFinder takes the data gathered from universites and aggregates it in an easy to understand, and useful format to help university students decide on the university of their choice.

Commissions

UFinder receives a commission from universites for students who register for their university using UFinder.

About Us

The Story of UFinder

UFinder was founded in September 2021, by a group of University students. The four of them shared a common interest in programming, and used to spend their wasted time on Reddit and Quora. As the opening date for University enrolment approached, they noticed that thousands of teenagers were seeking for help on these forums, on what would be the most suitable University for each of their situations. The University students found interesting the fact that these questions were essentially turned towards tuition fees, entry requirements, and ranking. Since most of these answers take their source directly on the institutions' websites, the University students decided to create a tool that would extract relevant information from the Internet to use it as a direct answer to the high school students' questions. After slight changes in the idea over time, this tool became UFinder, the application that allows students to find the Universities that match the most with their profile and ambitions.

Behind the scenes

In order to build the application, as well as the website to promote it, each member of the team has been assigned some tasks. For instance, while Lina had to edit the presentation video on Sony Vegas; Xavier, Maaz and Adam were coding different parts of the website, using HTML, CSS and Javascript. The work was coordinated and shared on GitHub. Weekly meetings on Microsoft Teams allowed the students to suggest modifications, to add features, and to help each other if needed.
